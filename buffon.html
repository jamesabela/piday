The Math

When needle length equals the gap between lines (L=D):

π ≈ 2N / C

Red Needles cross a line (C).
Blue Needles do not cross.

History

Proposed in 1777 by the Comte de Buffon. It was the first "Monte Carlo" method in history, using randomness to find Pi.
